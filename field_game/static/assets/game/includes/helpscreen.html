The game proceeds over 6 rounds. Each round consists of two decisions:

Decision 1: Treat your cows for infection

Tap on the hospital in the middle of the screen to 'treat' your cows, or tap on your farm to skip. This will cost 5 coins and it will make any infected cows healthy.

Healthy cows produce milk worth Â£1 per turn. Infected cows produce nothing.

Decision 2: who to buy from

Tap on the pen of another farm to bring one of their cows onto your own farm. 5 of your coins will go to the owner of the farm you chose. If your cows are treated you can only buy from other treated farms. If your cows are untreated, you can only choose farms that have untreated cows.

Tip: The higher the infection bar, the more likely an untreated cow you buy will be infected.

End of the round

At the end of the round two things happen:
  1. Infection spreads: the number of infected cows on a farm will double (until there are no more cows to become infected).
  2. Cows are milked: each farmer receives one coin for every healthy cow on their farm.

Winning the game

The winner of the game is the player who has the most money in the bank.

Infection

If the farmer does not treat their infected cows, it will spread to other cows in the pen.
