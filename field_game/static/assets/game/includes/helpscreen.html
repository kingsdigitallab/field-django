HOW TO PLAY

The game proceeds over 6 rounds. Each round consists of two decisions:


Decision 1: Treat your cows for infection

Tap the hospital to treat animals and make them healthy. Cost: 5 coins


OR

Tap your farmhouse to skip


Decision 2: who to buy from

Choose which farm to buy from by tapping their cow pen. Cows cost 5 coins.

You may only buy from farms with the same treatment status as you


End of the round

Income: Â£1 per healthy cow each round

Infection spreads: infected cows will infect other cows in the same pen

The bar in the top-right of the screen indicates the total number of sick cows across all the farms

Winning the game

The winner of the game is the player who has the most money after 6 rounds.

Infection

If the farmer does not treat their infected cows, it will spread to other cows in the pen.
